How Your Data is Stored
Stored Locally on Your Device
No internet or server required
All your transactions, stock records, and settings are saved directly in your browser's built-in database (IndexedDB) — the same technology used by apps like Google Docs for offline storage. Your data never leaves your device and requires no account or subscription.

⚠️ Important to know
• Data is stored on this device only — it won't sync across phones or computers automatically
• Clearing your browser's site data or cache will erase all records
• For backup: use the Export PDF button in Reports regularly
• Installing the app (PWA) helps protect data from accidental browser clears
